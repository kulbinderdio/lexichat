Local Area Checker

Local (UK) 100% local

Enter a postcode and get a sourced neighbourhood picture — it resolves the postcode to its ward, council and coordinates, then pulls crime, sold house prices, planning applications and local statistics, citing each figure.

Connects
Postcodes.io Police crime Land Registry prices Planning (PlanIt) Census / deprivation EPC · CQC (free key)
"Give me a full profile of SW1A 1AA."
Requires the Python code sandbox to be enabled (Admin → Tools) so it can tally large datasets accurately. Crime, prices, planning & stats are keyless; EPC and doctor/dentist ratings need a free account key.
