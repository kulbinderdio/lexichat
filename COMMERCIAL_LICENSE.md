# Commercial License

LexiChat is available under a dual-license model.

## When do you need a commercial license?

You need a commercial license if:

- You use LexiChat in a proprietary product or service (i.e. you do not release your source code under AGPLv3)
- You embed or integrate LexiChat into a commercial offering
- You provide LexiChat as a hosted or managed service
- Your organisation's legal policy prohibits use of AGPLv3-licensed software

## Personal and open-source use

LexiChat is free to use under the [AGPLv3](LICENSE) for:

- Personal, non-commercial use
- Open-source projects that are themselves licensed under AGPLv3 or a compatible license
